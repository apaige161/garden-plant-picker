About The Author

Alex Paige

I'm lazy, so naturally i tried to find an easier way to produce beautiful and delicous vegetables for a fraction of the work, water, space and most importantly, time.

Then I discovered square foot gardening, a more modern approach to gardening in small/medium yards. Ever since, I have been obssesing over how i can maximize yields in a small area to outproduce friends using traditional methods.

This web app came into existence due to a slow winter at work as an arborist. Lots of days off with no pay and no garden to play in. So I thought maybe I could build something to track my garden for next year. Six months down the rabbit hole of programming consuming almost all of my free time and finally I can share what I have done!

Also in case any potential employers are reading this... I am still an arborist looking for a full-stack position, preferably using the MEAN stack.
